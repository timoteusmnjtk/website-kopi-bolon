Grounded

There are several ways to make coffee. One of them is dripping liquid coffee juice, either by inserting coffee grounds into a coffee maker, or pouring hot water on coffee grounds that have been placed in a filter made of paper or iron. With coarser coffee grounds, you can use a French Press. With this method, you can mix water with the dregs, stir and after cooking for a few minutes, use a plunger to press down the dregs until it settles to the bottom of the container.
